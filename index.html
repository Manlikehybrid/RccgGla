Our Church

You Are Welcome
Just As You Are

RCCG Gospel Light Assembly is a Spirit-filled family in Lejina Phase 2, Ikorodu — a place where the broken are healed, the weary find rest, and lives are transformed by the power of God's Word.

Whether you've walked with God for decades or are taking your very first step of faith — there is a seat with your name on it. Come and experience the warmth of this family.

🔥

Faith Clinic

Evangelism

Worship & Praise
