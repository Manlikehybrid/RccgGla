Our Identity
✦

More Than a Church —
A Family of Believers

Gospel Light Assembly is a Zonal Headquarters of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), located in Lejina Phase 2, Adamo, Ikorodu, Lagos. We are a warm, Spirit-filled community dedicated to glorifying God and transforming our community through the power of the gospel.

We believe the church is not a building — it is people transformed by the love of Christ. Our mission is to carry the light of God into every home, street, and heart in Ikorodu and beyond.

  • Unwavering commitment to the authority of the Holy Bible
  • Prayer as the foundation of everything we do
  • Fervent evangelism and reaching the unreached
  • Discipleship and spiritual growth for every member
  • Genuine fellowship, love, and care for one another
  • Excellence in worship and service to God

Our Shepherd

Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Adebayo Erinle

Parish Pastor · RCCG Gospel Light Assembly

Pastor Adebayo Erinle leads Gospel Light Assembly with passion, humility, and a deep love for God and His people. Under his shepherding, the church has grown in faith, number, and community impact. He is committed to raising disciples who carry the light of the gospel into every area of life in Ikorodu and beyond.

"Come, let us walk together in the light of the Lord — there is room at this table for everyone."

Our Covering

Part of a Global Movement

Gospel Light Assembly is a proud Zonal Headquarters under the RCCG Zonal Headquarters. The Redeemed Christian Church of God, founded in 1952, now has a presence in over 197 countries with millions of members worldwide.

We are guided by the vision of General Overseer, Pastor E.A. Adeboye: "In every household in the whole world, there will be at least one member of the RCCG."
