Who We Are

A Family of Young Believers

The Youth Ministry of RCCG Gospel Light Assembly is for all young people aged 13–35. We are a community that takes faith seriously, but also knows how to have fun, build genuine friendships, and pursue God together.

We believe every young person has a God-given destiny waiting to be unlocked. Our mission is to create the environment where that happens — through the Word, through prayer, and through genuine community.

Youth Programmes

Bible Study (Digging Deep)

Every Tuesday at 5:30 PM — going deep into God's Word together. Iron sharpens iron.

Youth Prayer (Faith Clinic)

Thursdays at 5:30 PM — we believe in the power of young people praying together. Mountains move.
