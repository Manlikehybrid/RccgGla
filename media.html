About This Ministry

Media & Technical Unit

The Media & Technical Unit of Gospel Light Assembly is behind every broadcast, every captured moment, and every digital experience. We believe technology in the hands of God's people is a powerful instrument for the kingdom.

  • Excellence in every technical aspect of ministry
  • Professional quality in sound, video, and design
  • Live streaming every Sunday service
  • Social media presence that glorifies God
  • Continuous training and skill development
