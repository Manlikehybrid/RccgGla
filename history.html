The Beginning of a Dream

RCCG Gospel Light Assembly was founded with a singular vision: to bring the light of the gospel to the people of Adamo, Ikorodu, and the surrounding communities. What began as a small gathering of passionate believers has grown by the grace of God into a vibrant Zonal Headquarters that impacts lives every week.

The church is a proud parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), founded in Lagos, Nigeria in 1952 by Rev. Josiah Olufemi Akindayomi, and now led globally by Pastor E.A. Adeboye — one of the most influential Christian leaders in the world.

"Unless the Lord builds the house, the builders labour in vain." — Psalm 127:1

Our Timeline

The Vision

A Burden for Ikorodu

A group of faithful believers, burdened for the souls in Lejina Town and Adamo, began gathering for prayer and worship. From those early meetings, the vision for Gospel Light Assembly was born.

The Planting

Gospel Light Assembly is Established

The church was officially planted in Lejina Phase 2, Adamo, Ikorodu, under the covering of the RCCG. The name "Gospel Light Assembly" was chosen as a declaration — that this place would be a beacon of God's light in the community.

Growth

Expanding in Faith

The congregation grew steadily as more families joined and lives were transformed. Weekly programmes including Digging Deep (Bible Study) and the Faith Clinic (Prayer Service) were established and became pillars of the church's ministry.

Zonal HQ

Elevated to Zonal Headquarters

Through God's faithfulness and the church's consistent commitment to excellence, Gospel Light Assembly was elevated to a Zonal Headquarters — a recognition of its growth, impact, and leadership in the region.

Today

Shining Brighter Every Day

Under the shepherding of Pastor Adebayo Erinle, the church continues to grow in faith, numbers, and community impact. The light of God continues to shine — and there is no end in sight.

Our Connection to RCCG

We are part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God — a global movement that started in Lagos in 1952 and now has a presence in over 197 countries. RCCG's mission is that in every household in the whole world, there will be at least one member of the Redeemed Christian Church of God. Gospel Light Assembly is proud to be a carrier of that vision in Adamo, Ikorodu.
