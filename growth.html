Our Vision

The Church We Are Building

Gospel Light Assembly is on a journey of growth — not just in numbers, but in depth, impact, and facilities. We believe God has a magnificent future for this church and this community.

Our vision is to build a fully-equipped worship centre that can house our growing congregation, host community events, run a school, provide healthcare, and be a lighthouse of hope for all of Ikorodu.

  • A permanent, modern worship centre for our growing congregation
  • A children and youth facility fit for the next generation
  • A community hall for outreach, training, and social events
  • A counselling and welfare centre for the community
  • A media production studio for gospel broadcasting
  • Full accessibility for the elderly and disabled

Our Journey

Milestones of Faith

Foundation

Gospel Light Assembly Founded

A small group of believers with a big vision — to carry God's light to Adamo, Ikorodu and beyond. The church was established as a RCCG parish under the faithful leadership of Pastor Adebayo Erinle.

Growth

Ministry Units Established

Youth Ministry, Women's Fellowship, Men's Fellowship, Choir, Children's Ministry, Media Unit, Prayer Ministry, Evangelism Team, and Welfare Unit were all officially formed and activated.

Expansion

Zonal Headquarters Status

Gospel Light Assembly was designated as a Zonal Headquarters within the RCCG structure — a recognition of the church's growth, faithfulness, and leadership in the Ikorodu zone.

Digital

Online Presence Launched

The church website went live — connecting the congregation, reaching new visitors, and extending the ministry beyond our physical walls to every corner of Nigeria and the world.

Vision

The Dream Building

By God's grace and the generosity of His people, the permanent worship centre will be built — a beacon of hope in the heart of Ikorodu that will serve this community for generations to come.
