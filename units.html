About Our Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry of Gospel Light Assembly is a dynamic, Spirit-led community where young people aged 13–35 come to discover their God-given purpose, build lasting friendships, and grow deep in their walk with Christ. We believe in raising a generation of light-bearers for Ikorodu and beyond.
