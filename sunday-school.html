Why Sunday School Matters

"Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it." — Proverbs 22:6

Sunday School is the backbone of Biblical education in RCCG. It equips every member with foundational doctrine, builds community, and ensures that the Word of God takes deep root in every heart.
