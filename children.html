Our Heart for Children

Building Foundations That Last a Lifetime

The Children's Ministry of Gospel Light Assembly is dedicated to introducing children to Jesus Christ in a way that is fun, engaging, and transformative. We believe that no child is too young to encounter God's love.

Every Sunday, children learn Bible stories, memory verses, and life lessons through age-appropriate teaching, worship, and creative activities — all designed to build a lasting foundation of faith.

  • Safe, loving, and Spirit-filled environment
  • Age-appropriate Bible teaching every Sunday
  • Memory verse programme with rewards
  • Creative activities and Bible-based crafts
  • Trained, caring teachers and helpers
  • Special children's events and celebrations
